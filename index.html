Exercise

Your school wants to improve the upkeep of campus facilities by creating a new system for reporting any facilities that may need maintenance or repair. Design an experience that allows students to report building or equipment issues on campus. Consider the process of those filing the report and of those receiving and taking action on the issues.

My Process

For this problem, I've decided to look into the maintenance request system of Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT)â€”to evaluate it then build from it. The first step I took in approaching the design prompt was to identify the problem. "What did we have and what are the issues with it?" Answering these questions would help me build a better solution. To do this, I've evaluated the system in two ways: through contextual inquiry and heuristic evaluation. Ideally, the contextual inquiry would be conducted with more users and with input from those receiving the orders, however, I was unable to do this due to time constraint. After conducting one contextual inquiry and a heuristic evaluation of the system, I move on to step two.

Step two is where I started to create the solution. I gradually built up my prototype by creating a breadboard, followed by a wireframe, then the high-fidelity prototype. I designed the interfaces as much as I was able to, however, I was unable to create the prototype with transitions and effects.

Overall, I'm very proud of what I've accomplished and hope that it's sufficient as part of my application. I enjoyed the challenge and look forward to facing more of them soon.
